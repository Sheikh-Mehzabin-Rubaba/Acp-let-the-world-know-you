About Midnight Sun

Midnight Sun is a restaurant in Dhaka, Bangladesh that serves Chinese, Thai, and meat dishes.

Midnight Sun 1: Offers popular dishes like Tom Kha Gal, Thai Style Chicken, and Grilled Chicken. Some say the food is good but the prices can be high.

Midnight Sun 2: Located at 3/B Mirpur Road New Market, Dhaka City 1205. Some popular menu items include Midnight Sun Special Fried Chicken, Thai Style Chicken, and Grilled Chicken.

Midnight Sun 3: Offers delivery through foodpanda.
